Welcome to your Nampa terminal.

Providing for your transportation needs from La Grande, OR, across southern Idaho, to Green River, WY.
We welcome both passengers and/or cargo with optimal comfort and care, at an affordable price.


Our mission is to help reduce the number of vehicles in use across western Oregon, eastern Wyoming and all of Southern Idaho.
Our goal would limit fossil fuel consumption and preserve our air quaility across all our communities.
As a reliable and safe form of transportation, riding in luxury also reduces accident risk in inclement weather. We look forward to serving you. Come travel with us today.
